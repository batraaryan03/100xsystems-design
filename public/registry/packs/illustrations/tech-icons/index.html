Usage

All icons are standard SVGs on a 24×24 viewBox. Copy the SVG element directly into your HTML or React component. Change the stroke or fill to match your brand colors. Adjust stroke-width for thinner/thicker lines.

<!-- React icon --> <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="#61dafb" stroke-width="1.5" stroke-linecap="round" stroke-linejoin="round"> <circle cx="12" cy="12" r="2.5"/> <ellipse cx="12" cy="12" rx="9" ry="4" transform="rotate(0 12 12)"/> <ellipse cx="12" cy="12" rx="9" ry="4" transform="rotate(60 12 12)"/> <ellipse cx="12" cy="12" rx="9" ry="4" transform="rotate(120 12 12)"/> </svg>
